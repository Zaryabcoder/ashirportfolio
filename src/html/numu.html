About Me

Numu

An app that incentivizes social media users in the form of discounts and promos, based on their following.

Context

I was hired by a Melbourne based start-up NumuWorld to design an application targeted at social media savvy young adults. It aimed at providing incentives to the user in the form of discounts and promos based on their influence, and businesses such as coffee shops, restaurants and clothing stores more reach in the form of marketing.

The Idea

Whenever a social media user mentions a certain business on their social media, they get certain points for that against which they can redeem discounts from certain businesses which are on-board with Numu. The businesses in return get marketing and outreach to a wider audience through their clients.

Process

In the start, I finalized the requirements of the project - eliciting the functionalities and the users. The solution identified two type of users: the business and their clients (social media users). Our main focus was on the client side of the app. I created user flows on the basis of the requirements and scenarios and came up with the suitable solution for the app’s information architecture and navigation schema. After that, I designed the wireframes in Balsamiq and lastly the mockups for the whole app on Photoshop.
